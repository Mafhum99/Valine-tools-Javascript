Polar to Cartesian Converter

Convert polar coordinates to Cartesian coordinates with multiple angle unit options

Formulas:
x = r × cos(θ)
y = r × sin(θ)

Angle unit conversions to radians:
Degrees: θ(rad) = θ(°) × π/180
Radians: θ(rad) = θ
Gradians: θ(rad) = θ(grad) × π/200
Turns: θ(rad) = θ(turns) × 2π
