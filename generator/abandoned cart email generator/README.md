# Abandoned Cart Email Generator

> **Category**: Marketing Tools
> **Icon**: 🛒
> **Type**: Generator

## 📝 Description
**How it works:** Generate a complete abandoned cart email sequence with 4 recovery emails, including subject lines, preview text, and body content. Enter your store or brand name to get personalized email templates ready for your e-commerce campaigns.

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Enter your store or brand name
3. Click Generate Emails button
4. View results instantly
5. Copy results if needed
