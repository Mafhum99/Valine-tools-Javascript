Ellipse Equation Calculator

Find the equation of an ellipse from center and axis lengths

Horizontal Major Axis (a ≥ b):
(x - h)²/a² + (y - k)²/b² = 1

Vertical Major Axis (a ≥ b):
(x - h)²/b² + (y - k)²/a² = 1

Where: (h, k) = center, a = semi-major axis, b = semi-minor axis
