# Sleep Calculator

Determine the best time to sleep or wake up based on 90-minute sleep cycles.

## Features
- Calculate optimal sleep or wake-up times
- Accounts for 15 minutes average time to fall asleep
- Recommends 4, 5, or 6 sleep cycles (6h, 7.5h, 9h)
- Mobile-friendly design
- Copy results to clipboard

## Usage
1. Choose whether you want to calculate based on a wake-up time or a bedtime.
2. Enter the time.
3. Click **Calculate Best Times**.
