# ABC Analysis Calculator

> **Category**: Ecommerce Tools
> **Icon**: 📁
> **Type**: Calculator

## 📝 Description
**How it works:** Enter percentage of A and B items and their revenue share to perform ABC inventory classification. The calculator computes the remaining C category automatically, helping you prioritize inventory management based on the Pareto principle.

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Enter the percentage of A items and their revenue share
3. Enter the percentage of B items and their revenue share
4. Click Calculate button
5. View results instantly
6. Copy results if needed
